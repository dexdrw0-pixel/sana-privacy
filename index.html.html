Privacy Policy

📅 Last updated: June 2025 🌍 Applies to all Sana users

Introduction

Sana is an AI-powered virtual receptionist designed for dental and medical clinics. Sana communicates with patients via WhatsApp to assist with appointment booking, reminders, and general clinic inquiries.

This Privacy Policy explains what personal data Sana collects, how it is used, and what rights you have regarding your information. By interacting with Sana through WhatsApp or any other channel, you agree to the practices described here.

Data We Collect

When you interact with Sana, the following information may be collected:

Identity
Your name as provided during conversation
Contact
WhatsApp phone number used to message Sana
Appointment Data
Requested dates, times, and type of visit
Messages
Content of your conversations with Sana
Clinic Association
Which clinic you are booking with
Metadata
Message timestamps and delivery status
⚕️ Sana does not collect medical diagnoses, prescriptions, or clinical health records. All health-related discussions remain between you and your doctor.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to deliver Sana's receptionist services:

  • Booking, confirming, and managing appointments on your behalf
  • Sending appointment reminders and follow-up messages via WhatsApp
  • Answering questions about clinic services, availability, and location
  • Improving the accuracy and performance of Sana's responses
  • Complying with legal obligations if required by law

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or sell it to any third party.

Third-Party Services

Sana operates using the following trusted third-party platforms to function:

  • Meta (WhatsApp Business API) — the messaging channel through which Sana operates. Meta's own Privacy Policy applies to message delivery.
  • Google Calendar / Google Sheets — used to store and manage appointment data securely.
  • n8n (Automation Platform) — the backend automation engine that processes your messages and triggers booking workflows.
  • OpenAI / AI Language Model — used to understand and generate natural language responses. No personal data is stored by the AI provider beyond the session.

Each of these providers operates under their own privacy standards and data protection agreements.

Data Retention

Appointment and contact data is retained for as long as necessary to provide the service to your clinic, or as required by applicable healthcare regulations in your country.

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting the clinic directly or reaching out via the contact information below.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction — ask us to correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion — request that your data be erased ("right to be forgotten")
  • Objection — object to how your data is being processed
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details in the section below.

Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. All data transmitted via WhatsApp is protected by Meta's end-to-end encryption on the user side.

Access to backend systems (n8n, Google Sheets) is restricted to authorized personnel only.
